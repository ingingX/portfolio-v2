# Projects

## Portfolio
---

My projects explored trustworthy and human-centered AI systems for complex real-world environments, combining machine learning, AI systems engineering, and interdisciplinary research perspectives.

My research trajectory began with communication engineering, signal processing, and information theory, which led me to early research experiences in computer vision and visual saliency analysis. Through subsequent work on human versus neural attention in vision-language models, I became increasingly interested in explainable AI, human-AI interaction, and the interpretability of modern deep learning systems.

Alongside academic research, my industrial experience in AI deployment, digitalization, and intelligent system integration further strengthened my interest in building deployable and reliable AI systems that can effectively support human decision-making in practical environments.

The projects presented here reflect my ongoing interests in trustworthy AI, AI-assisted reasoning, intelligent monitoring systems, representation learning, multimodal AI evaluation, and applied machine learning for cybersecurity, scientific, and industrial applications.
